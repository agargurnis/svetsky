SVETSKY

Svetsky is an Interior Design studio based in London, offering bespoke furniture packages and interior design services. Our team offers services including mood board creation, interior design and colour consultation, styling and sourcing unusual pieces. We also provide furnishing packages by putting together a collection of carefully curated furnishing schemes to suit the majority of properties.
