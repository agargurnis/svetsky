FURNITURE PACKAGES

We understand how important it is to make a first impression sometimes, which is why our customised furniture packages are created to give your property maximum appeal with less amount of hassle. Our furniture packages have been designed as a full furnishing solution which includes everything required for immediate occupation.

For each furniture piece within our package there are multiple choices on offer, including 2 seater and L-shape sofas, upholstered beds and statement dining furniture. We also offer additional styling packs which include artwork and decorative accessories, plus bespoke window treatments to compliment a furniture package you have selected for your property.

Your furniture package will be delivered, arranged, installed and tested into your property to give you and your tenant complete peace of mind and total satisfaction.

All pieces include VAT, delivery, installation, post installation clean and a two year warranty.
